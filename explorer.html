How the concierge works

Three gentle steps, then you just show up and live the story.

1 · Share your rhythm

Tell us who’s coming, your rough dates, budget comfort and what Pondy means to you – cafés, waves, quiet corners, or all of it.

2 · We curate the puzzle

We map you to stays, transfers, experiences and food trails – always starting from the most value‑for‑money verified partners.

3 · You review & confirm

You see a clean itinerary and starting price, tweak a few knobs, then confirm and pay securely or request a call for human fine‑tuning.
