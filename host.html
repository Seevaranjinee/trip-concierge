Become part of Pondy’s story

Pondy Curations connects explorers with a small, carefully verified circle of stays, cafés, tour operators, wellness spaces and experience creators. If you bring warmth, quality and a touch of French‑Indian charm, this is for you.

Why list with Pondy Curations?

This isn’t a mass marketplace. It’s a small, high‑trust circle where each partner is known by name and every recommendation is intentional.

  • Explorers arrive primed for quality, not just the lowest deal.
  • We tell your story – not just your price – through copy and curated placements.
  • We help match you to the right kind of guests (quiet couples, families, workations, etc.).
  • You get a real human to discuss pricing, visibility and feedback loops.

What kind of hosts we onboard

We are especially excited about:

  • Heritage villas, boutique hotels and character‑rich homestays.
  • Cafés, bistros and cloud kitchens with a strong point of view.
  • Guides, storytellers, photographers and wellness practitioners.
  • Transport and logistics partners who care about reliability and safety.

If you bring a slice of Pondicherry’s soul to guests, there’s space for you here.
